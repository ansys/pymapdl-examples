.. _ref_vm_examples:

===================
Verification Manual
===================
This section demonstrates a range of Mechanical APDL elements and capabilities
in straightforward problems which have “classical” or readily-obtainable
theoretical solutions using PyMAPDL.

These problems may then serve as the basis for additional validation and
qualification of Mechanical APDL capabilities for specific applications that
may be of interest.
